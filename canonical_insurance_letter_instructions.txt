CANONICAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR INSURANCE LETTER GENERATION
==========================================================================================

1. **Header Section**: Include the company name, address, and date at the top of the letter.
   - Example: 
     ```
     [Company Name]
     [Company Address]
     [City, State, Zip Code]
     [Date]
     ```

2. **Subject Line**: Clearly state the purpose of the letter.
   - Example: 
     ```
     Subject: [Type of Letter: Denial Letter / Coverage Decision / Request for Additional Information]
     ```

3. **Salutation**: Address the recipient by name.
   - Example: 
     ```
     Dear [Recipient's Name],
     ```

4. **Claim Information**: Reference the specific insurance policy and claim number.
   - Example: 
     ```
     We are writing regarding your insurance policy [Policy Number] and claim number [Claim Number].
     ```

5. **Claim Review Summary**: Briefly summarize the review process or current status of the claim.
   - Example: 
     ```
     Claim Review Summary
     --------------------
     [For Denial Letter] We carefully reviewed your claim submission and supporting documentation.
     [For Coverage Decision] We have completed a comprehensive review of your claim, including all documentation provided and the applicable provisions of your insurance policy.
     [For Request for Additional Information] We are currently unable to complete our evaluation of your claim due to missing information.
     ```

6. **Coverage Determination**: Clearly state the decision regarding coverage or the information requested.
   - Example: 
     ```
     Coverage Determination
     ----------------------
     [For Denial Letter] After evaluation, we regret to inform you that coverage cannot be extended for this claim based on policy exclusions and conditions.
     [For Coverage Decision] Based on this review, coverage has been approved under the terms, conditions, and limitations of your policy. Any applicable deductibles or limits will apply.
     ```

7. **Your Options / Next Steps**: Outline the options available to the recipient following the denial or the next steps they should take.
   - Example: 
     ```
     Your Options / Next Steps
     --------------------------
     [For Denial Letter] - You may request a written explanation of this decision.
     - You may submit additional documentation for reconsideration.
     - Appeal rights are outlined in your policy.
     
     [For Coverage Decision] - Payment, if applicable, will be processed in accordance with standard timelines.
     - You will receive separate confirmation once payment is issued.
     - Retain this letter for your records.
     
     [For Request for Additional Information] Please submit the requested information within the specified timeframe to avoid delays.
     ```

8. **Compliance and Regulatory Notice**: Include compliance language regarding the decision or request.
   - Example: 
     ```
     Compliance and Regulatory Notice
     This correspondence is issued in accordance with applicable insurance laws and regulations. All determinations are based on the terms, conditions, exclusions, and limitations of your policy.
     You have the right to request policy provisions, submit additional documentation, or appeal this decision as permitted by governing law. Applicable deadlines are outlined in your policy.
     ```

9. **Contact Information**: Provide a way for the recipient to reach out with questions.
   - Example: 
     ```
     If you have questions regarding this correspondence, please contact our Claims Department at [Phone Number] within thirty (30) days of receipt of this notice.
     ```

10. **Closing**: End the letter with a formal closing and the name of the department.
    - Example: 
      ```
      Sincerely,
      Claims Department
      [Company Name]
      ```
